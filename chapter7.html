Chapter 7: Multimedia

Introduction to Multimedia

Multimedia combines text, images, audio, video, and animation to create interactive content. This chapter explains the basics of multimedia and its significance.

Components of Multimedia

  • Text
  • Images
  • Audio
  • Video
  • Animation

Applications of Multimedia

Multimedia is used in education, advertising, entertainment, and many other fields to enhance communication and user engagement.
