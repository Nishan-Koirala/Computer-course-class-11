Chapter 8: Information Security & Cyber Law

Introduction to Information Security

Information security involves protecting digital data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. This section covers the principles and techniques used to safeguard information.

Cyber Law

Cyber Law deals with legal issues related to the digital world, including privacy, intellectual property, and cybercrime. It is essential for protecting users and organizations in a connected environment.

Applications and Challenges

This section discusses the measures taken to secure information and the challenges posed by modern cyber threats.
