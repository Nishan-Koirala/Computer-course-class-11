Chapter 4: Application Package

MS-Word (Word Processor)

MS-Word is used for creating, editing, and formatting documents. It provides features such as spell checking, text formatting, graphics insertion, and mail merge. Documents are saved with .doc or .docx extensions.

MS-Excel (Spreadsheet)

MS-Excel is a spreadsheet application that organizes, analyzes, and visualizes data. It supports calculations, chart creation, and data management. Files are saved with .xls or .xlsx extensions.

MS-PowerPoint (Presentation)

MS-PowerPoint enables the creation of professional presentations with slides that include text, images, charts, and multimedia. Presentations are saved with .ppt or .pptx extensions.
